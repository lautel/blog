About me

Hi, I am Laura Cabello. I recently completed a Masters degree in Telecommunication Engineering at the University of Zaragoza. Currently I am working as a researcher within the audio group at my hometown university. My principal research interests are signal processing, audio technologies and natural language processing (NLP). And I love when they help to solve real, human problems.

I am a curious person and I do enjoy traveling the world. All these experiences shape who I am today and help me to gain a richer understanding of life.
